# Simple Solutions — Full Product Suite: Competitive Analysis, Pricing & Profitability Model

## Context
Simple Solutions offers four integrated products for small businesses. This document analyzes each against the competition, recommends pricing, and projects profitability up to 2,000+ customers with all possible product combinations.

# COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

## Product 1: Managed Website Service

### What You Offer
- Free website build (AI-assisted, fast turnaround)
- $49/mo management (edits, updates, maintenance)
- $79/mo+ with upgrades (chatbot, faster edits, lead gen, AI integration)

### Competition

| Competitor | Price | Model |
|---|---|---|
| B12.io | $49-$399/mo | Done-for-you + AI-assisted build |
| Wix/Squarespace | $10-$52/mo | DIY self-serve (no management) |
| Duda (agencies) | $19-$149/mo | White-label for agencies |
| Hibu | $449-$10,000/mo | Full agency service |
| BentoBox (restaurants) | $119-$479/mo | Restaurant-specific |
| Popmenu (restaurants) | $149-$249/mo | Restaurant-specific |
| Typical agency care plan | $100-$500/mo | Maintenance + edits |

### Your Advantage
- **Free build is a massive hook.** B12 charges $49/mo minimum even to start. Wix/Squarespace require the customer to build it themselves. You eliminate both barriers.
- **$49/mo management undercuts the agency standard** ($100-$500/mo for comparable service).
- **$79/mo with chatbot/AI bundles what others sell separately.** Tidio alone costs $29-$59/mo. ChatBot.com starts at $52/mo. You're bundling that into a $30 upgrade.

### Recommended Pricing

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|---|---|---|
| Free Website | $0 | Build + host. Self-managed. Marketing funnel entry point. |
| Managed | $49/mo | Edits, updates, maintenance, hosting, SSL, basic support |
| Managed Pro | $79/mo | Everything above + AI chatbot, faster edit turnaround (24hr), lead capture forms |
| Managed Premium | $129/mo | Everything above + monthly SEO report, Google Business integration, priority support, restaurant-specific features (menu updates, ordering links) |

**Why $129 Premium:** BentoBox charges $119/mo for a basic restaurant website alone. Popmenu charges $149/mo. At $129 with chatbot + SEO + fast edits, you undercut the restaurant-specific players while serving ALL verticals.

## Product 2: Accounts Receivable & Cash Flow

### What You Offer
- Collect past-due invoices, take 8% of recovered amount
- Cash flow smoothing and prediction

### Competition

| Competitor | Fee | Model |
|---|---|---|
| Collection agencies (<90 days) | 10-25% | Contingency on recovered amount |
| Collection agencies (90 days-1yr) | 25-40% | Contingency |
| Collection agencies (1yr+) | 30-50% | Contingency |
| Chaser (AR automation) | $49-$369/mo | Flat monthly SaaS fee |
| InvoiceSherpa | $49-$199/mo | Flat monthly SaaS fee |
| BILL (Bill.com) | $45-$89/user/mo | AP/AR automation platform |
| Invoice factoring | 1-5%/month of invoice value | Sell invoices at discount |
| Float (cash flow prediction) | $59-$199/mo | Standalone forecasting tool |

### Your Advantage
- **8% is 2-5x cheaper than ANY collection agency.** The cheapest agencies charge 10-25% for fresh receivables. Most charge 25-40%.
- **Zero risk to the customer.** No monthly fee, no minimum. You only get paid when they get paid. AR SaaS tools charge $49-$369/mo whether they collect anything or not.
- **Cash flow prediction bundled in** — Float charges $59-$199/mo for this alone as a standalone product.
- **56% of small businesses are owed money** from unpaid invoices. Average owed: $17,000-$17,500. Average annual cost of late payments: $39,000-$40,000/business.

### Recommended Pricing

| Component | Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Past-due collection | 8% of recovered amount | Zero upfront cost. Industry standard is 25-40%. This is your killer differentiator. |
| Cash flow dashboard + prediction | Included with AR service | Bundled value. Competitors charge $59-$199/mo separately. |
| Optional: Proactive AR management | $49/mo flat | Automated reminders, aging reports, payment tracking BEFORE invoices go past due. Competes with Chaser ($49/mo starter). |

### Opportunity: Tiered Collection Rates
- 0-60 days past due: 8% (your hook rate — massively undercuts everyone)
- 61-120 days: 12% (still half of industry standard 25%)
- 121+ days: 18% (still half of industry standard 35-50%)

A business with $17,000 in past-due invoices collected at 8% = $1,360 to you. At tiered rates averaging 12% across age buckets = $2,040. Over 2,000 customers that difference is significant.

# OPPORTUNITIES

## 1. The Free Website as a Data Moat
Every free website you build gives GEO more data to train on. At 1,000 free websites, GEO has visibility into traffic patterns, customer behavior, and business performance across industries. This makes GEO's recommendations better for everyone — a network effect.

## 2. AR Cross-Sell is Almost Automatic
When GEO sees a customer's cash flow dipping, it can recommend the AR service. The product sells itself through the AI mentor. This is a built-in upsell engine.

## 3. Website to AR Pipeline
When you manage a business's website, you already see their traffic and can infer revenue patterns. Offering AR services to managed website customers is a natural conversation.

## 4. Industry-Specific Website Templates as Vertical Play
Restaurants, contractors, salons, medical practices — each has specific website needs. Building industry templates means faster builds (lower cost to you) and higher perceived value.

## 5. Referral Revenue from AR
Businesses whose invoices you collect become advocates. Consider a referral bonus ($50-$100 credit) to accelerate word-of-mouth.

## 6. GEO as a Retention Lock
Once a business owner relies on GEO for decisions — and GEO has months of their data, trends, and context — switching costs are enormous. This is the stickiest product in the suite.

## 7. NanoClaw Integration = Automation Premium
When ready: NanoClaw could automate website content updates, AR follow-up sequences, appointment reminders, social media posting. $50-$100/mo add-on or built into Enterprise tier.

## 8. White-Label / Agency Partner Channel
Other agencies and freelancers could use your platform to manage their clients' websites. A white-label tier at $29-$49/mo per client site opens a B2B2C channel.
